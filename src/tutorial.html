Getting Started

How To Start?
  • A computer with Chrome or Firefox browser installed.
  • Install and register MetaMast Wallet. All of our game process is given to MetaMast. You need to make sure the Chrome or Firefox browser is installed and logged into your wallet.
  • The involvement of the TotalGame is based on ETH.
How to install MetaMast?
  • Go to the MetaMask official website , add it to your browser, or go to the Google Chrome Store, find the MetaMast plugin, and click "Add to Chrome"
  • Set up your MetaMask account and password.
  • Copy 12 mnemonics and place them in safe place. Recovery accounts need to useit. Please save your mnemonics properly. It is equivalent to the key. If leaked, you will lose all funds.
  • For security purposes, the MetaMask plugin sometimes asks you to log in again. If you see a lock screen while in the TotalGame, it may cause an error in the purchase operation and the "Personal Center" page. You just need to log in again to continue the game and browse.
What is TotalGame ?
  • TotalGame's first season games include mainstream sports events around the world. For example, each team in the World Cup or five major leagues perteam. Every team has a smart contract.If you buy it, You will be its owner. Each team can only have one owner. Every victory for your team, the owner of the team will receive a dividend of %1 on the match’s total betting value. E.g:

    The match's total betting value is 100ETH. After the team's victory, the team's owner will receive 1 ETH.

  • Anyone can buy a team you own as long as they pay you a new price，the price is calculated from a smart contract.
  • If you are not the owner of the team, you can also participate in the game by guessing game results.
  • The price increase of the team follows the following pattern:

    Price level Price range Increase rate Fees

    L1 0.001 ETH - 0.02 ETH 100% 5%

    L2 0.02 ETH - 0.5 ETH 35% 4%

    L3 0.5 ETH - 2.0 ETH 25% 3%

    L4 2.0 ETH - 5.0 ETH 17% 3%

    L5 5.0 ETH - 15% 2%

About odds and point spreads?
  • Each team will present his success rate to the user based on his previous record.
  • Odds will vary dynamically depending on the way players bet.
  • For example: In a game, more people like the host team. (more bets), the host teamodds are low.and the odds of the visiting team (sports) are high, and vice versa.
  • Every match has an associated point spread. This is a positive or negative number that is added to the ﬁnal score of teamA (the home team) prior to evaluating the outcome of a match. This is done so that even teams with diﬀerent skill levels can be traded at close to even odds, and also so that ties (“pushes”) cannot occur (because in most sports scores are integers but point spreads have fractional components). TotalGame chooses its point-spreads based oﬀ the initially posted vegas or oﬀ-shore lines.
Does TotalGame issue cryptocurrencies?
  • Yes, in the game, when you bet a match through a smart contract, the system will distribute additional open-source currency TotalGame Coin(TGC) according to a specific ratio.
  • In the follow-up, We will develop more games and you can use TGC to participate. Looking forward to it!
How to buy a team?
  • Select the "Buy" button below the team that will be purchased
  • Click "SUBMIT" button in the Metamask popup dialog.
  • After the purchase is successful, the system prompts "Congratulations! You have bought a team now!"
  • You can view your team through the "Mine" page.
